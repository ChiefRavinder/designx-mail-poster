🎨Design Guidelines

Objective: This competition aims to encourage you to use your artistic talent to design posters that honor and celebrate the rich heritage and culture of indigenous tribes on the occasion of World Tribal Day.

Submission Deadline: The deadline for poster submissions is set for 18 August 2023 EOD.

Poster Size: The dimensions for the poster should be 1080 x 1350 (4:5 ratio). Please ensure that your design adheres to these measurements.

Necessary Information: Your poster design should capture the essence of World Tribal Day and include elements that highlight the significance of this event. Feel free to incorporate relevant imagery, symbols, and text.

Design Guidelines: We invite you to let your creativity run wild and create posters that genuinely reflect the vibrancy, diversity, and uniqueness of indigenous cultures. While there are no strict design guidelines, please ensure that the key information about World Tribal Day is legible and well-presented. All the designs will be posted on DesignX instagram handle.

Judging Criteria: The judging process will take into account both Instagram likes and the evaluation by our panel of judges. The judging criteria will be as follows:

Instagram Likes: 30%

Evaluation by DesignX-Designing Club Panel: 70%

 Prize Money: INR 3000  ( 1st: INR 1200, 2nd: INR 1000, 3rd: INR 800)

 

Disqualification: Submit the raw file (.ai or .psd , etc.) or a direct link to your original work (canva) to maintain fairness and transparency. Non-compliance may lead to disqualification, upholding evaluation integrity. Any designs that are found to depict vulgarity or inappropriate content will result in immediate disqualification of the respective entry.
